# Sarah Ashley - Profile Addendum (scoring context)

This supplements the handbook with current targeting and scoring guidance. Where
this conflicts with the handbook, this addendum wins.

## Updated Targeting

- Lead with **Customer Onboarding / Implementation** roles. The 60-to-30-day
  time-to-value result is the anchor story.

- Open to **Program / Operations Manager** roles at growth-stage B2B SaaS

- Roles requiring SQL proficiency or data engineering.
- Roles requiring software engineering or coding.
- Director/VP level at companies with 200+ employees.
- Deep domain expertise required in healthcare, fintech, or cybersecurity.
- Roles requiring 10+ years of dedicated experience in one narrow function.

## Unique Match Signals (flag positively)

- "Build from scratch" or "first hire" language.
- Cross-functional coordination emphasis.
- Growth-stage companies (30-150 people).
- AI-forward companies deploying AI to non-technical users.
- Process improvement and documentation focus.

Target ~$100-120K CAD salaried. Flag clearly-below-range roles, but a strong
company at the right level is worth considering near the range.

## Location

Based in Calgary, AB. Remote-Canada or Remote-North-America preferred. Vancouver
or Toronto hybrid is possible but not preferred. Do not lower the numeric score
for an acceptable remote role; reflect location in the location field.
