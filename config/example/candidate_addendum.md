# Sarah Ashley - Profile Addendum (scoring context)

This supplements the handbook with current targeting and scoring guidance. Where
this conflicts with the handbook, this addendum wins.

## Updated Targeting

- Lead with **Nurse Educator / Clinical Nurse Educator** roles. The
  lead-preceptor and new-grad-residency experience is the anchor story.
- **Clinical Coordinator** is a strong secondary target.
- Open to **Professional Development / Nurse Residency Coordinator** and
  **Charge Nurse / Assistant Nurse Manager** roles on med-surg or telemetry units.

## Hard Blockers (cap the score at 4)

- Roles requiring an active NP, CNS, or other advanced-practice license.
- Roles requiring a graduate degree (MSN/DNP required, not preferred).
- ICU, ER, OR, L&D, or other specialty roles requiring direct experience she
  lacks.
- Faculty roles requiring prior academic/nursing-school teaching experience.
- Roles requiring 10+ years in one narrow specialty.

## Unique Match Signals (flag positively)

- Preceptor, orientation, or nurse-residency program language.
- Med-surg or telemetry focus.
- Epic / EHR super-user or documentation-training emphasis.
- Shared-governance, evidence-based-practice, or staff-development culture.
- Magnet-designated or teaching hospitals.

## Compensation

Target ~$80-105K USD salaried. Flag clearly-below-range roles, but a strong
employer at the right level is worth considering near the range.

## Location

Based in Denver, CO. Prefers roles within the Denver metro; open to relocation
within Colorado. Some travel between hospital campuses is fine. Reflect location
fit in the location field rather than lowering the numeric score for an
acceptable local role.
